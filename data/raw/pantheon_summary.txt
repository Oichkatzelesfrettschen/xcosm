================================================================================
INTERPRETATION
================================================================================

PRIMARY CONCLUSION:
The Pantheon+ data does NOT support the strong Hubble bifurcation hypothesis.
The observed ΔH₀ = 0.34 km/s/Mpc is ~12× smaller than the predicted 4.0 km/s/Mpc
and is not statistically significant.

POSSIBLE EXPLANATIONS:

1. STANDARDIZATION SUCCESS
   The SALT3 light curve fitting procedure already accounts for stretch-dependent
   luminosity variations, effectively removing any intrinsic H₀-x₁ correlation.

2. BIAS CORRECTIONS APPLIED
   Pantheon+ includes sophisticated bias corrections (biasCor_m_b in the data)
   that may already correct for population-dependent systematic effects.

3. REDSHIFT EFFECTS
   The hint of signal at z = 0.07-0.10 (ΔH₀ = 3.52 km/s/Mpc) suggests possible
   redshift-dependent effects, but requires larger samples for confirmation.

4. METHODOLOGY LIMITATIONS
   The simple H₀ = cz/d_L approximation may not be sensitive enough to detect
   subtle population differences that require full cosmological modeling.

5. HYPOTHESIS REFINEMENT NEEDED
   The x₁ parameter may not be the optimal proxy for fractal dimension D in
   the context of already-standardized SNe Ia data.

================================================================================
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR FUTURE ANALYSIS
================================================================================

1. RE-ANALYZE RAW LIGHT CURVES
   Fit light curves WITHOUT stretch corrections to see raw x₁ dependence

2. FULL COSMOLOGICAL FITTING
   Use MCMC to fit separate H₀ values for each population with proper
   cosmological distance-redshift relation (including Ω_M, Ω_Λ)

3. FOCUS ON VERY LOW-z
   Restrict to z < 0.03 where H₀ = cz/d_L approximation is most valid

4. INVESTIGATE 0.07-0.10 BIN
   Larger sample in this specific redshift range could confirm/refute the
   observed 3.5 km/s/Mpc signal

5. ALTERNATIVE D PROXIES
   Explore other fractal dimension indicators beyond SALT3 x₁ parameter

6. EXAMINE STANDARDIZATION
   Investigate whether SALT3 stretch correction itself removes the bifurcation
